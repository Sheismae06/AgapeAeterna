About

Agape Aeterna was born from a deep love for fragrances — not just any scent, but those that last, linger, and leave a mark. For me, perfume is more than just a fragrance. It is a reflection of one’s personality, a quiet statement, and a lasting memory left behind.

The name Agape Aeterna means eternal, unconditional love — a reflection of who I am and what I want to share with the world. Through this brand, I want people to feel loved, valued, and confident. I want each scent to give them the power to walk into any room and be remembered — even without saying a word.

Mission

To craft a variety of signature scents that celebrate different personalities — making each one unforgettable, lovable, and soulfully created for everyone.

Vision

To become a trusted name in local fragrance — known for soulful blends, high-quality ingredients, and affordable luxury. We aim to build a community where every bottle tells a story, and where scent becomes a symbol of self-expression, confidence, and love.
