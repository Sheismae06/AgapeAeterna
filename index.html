UNDERSTANDING PERFUME CONCENTRATIONS

Parfum / Extrait de Parfum: 20–30% — 8–12+ hours — Deep, intense, and luxurious. Best for special occasions.
Eau de Parfum (EDP): 15–20% — 6–8 hours — Balanced and long-lasting. Ideal for daily wear and evenings.
Eau de Toilette (EDT): 5–15% — 4–6 hours — Fresh, lighter scent. Good for hot weather or casual days.
Eau de Cologne (EDC): 2–5% — 2–4 hours — Very light. Great for layering or quick refresh.
Body Mist / Splash: 1–3% — 1–2 hours — Gentle scent. Best for post-shower or bedtime.

PERFUME STORAGE TIPS

✅ Store in a cool, dry place (away from sunlight and heat).
❌ Don’t leave perfumes in humid areas like bathrooms.
✅ Keep the cap tightly closed to prevent evaporation.
❄️ Optional: Refrigerate only if your room gets too warm — but avoid temperature changes.

HOW TO MAKE PERFUME LAST LONGER

ON SKIN: Apply on pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears, inner elbows). Moisturize first with unscented lotion or petroleum jelly. Don’t rub wrists together — it breaks top notes. Spray from 6–8 inches for better diffusion.
ON CLOTHES: Spray on fabric-safe areas like collars or sleeves. Avoid delicate fabrics like silk — test first. Let air dry naturally — no rubbing or patting.

BONUS TIPS

Spray a bit in your hairbrush (not directly on hair). Layer using matching body lotions or oils. Carry a travel-size atomizer for touch-ups.
