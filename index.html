UNDERSTANDING PERFUME CONCENTRATIONS

Parfum / Extrait de Parfum — 20–30% oil, 8–12+ hrs, deep and luxurious.
Eau de Parfum — 15–20%, 6–8 hrs, balanced, good for daily wear.
Eau de Toilette — 5–15%, 4–6 hrs, lighter and fresh.
Eau de Cologne — 2–5%, 2–4 hrs, very light and refreshing.
Body Mist — 1–3%, 1–2 hrs, gentle and best for layering or after shower.

PERFUME STORAGE TIPS

✅ Store in a cool, dry place (away from sunlight and heat).
❌ Don’t leave perfumes in humid areas like bathrooms.
✅ Keep the cap tightly closed to prevent evaporation.
❄️ Optional: Refrigerate only if your room gets too warm — but avoid sudden changes.

HOW TO MAKE PERFUME LAST LONGER

On Skin: Apply to pulse points, moisturize first, don’t rub wrists together.
On Clothes: Spray on fabric-safe areas, let air dry naturally, avoid delicate fabrics.
Bonus Tips: Spray on hairbrush, layer with body lotions, carry travel atomizer.
