About

Agape Aeterna was born from a deep love for fragrances — not just any scent, but those that last, linger, and leave a mark. For me, perfume is more than just a fragrance. It is a reflection of one’s personality, a quiet statement, and a lasting memory left behind.

The name Agape Aeterna means eternal, unconditional love — a reflection of who I am and what I want to share with the world. Through this brand, I want people to feel loved, valued, and confident. I want each scent to give them the power to walk into any room and be remembered — even without saying a word.

Mission

To craft a variety of signature scents that celebrate different personalities — making each one unforgettable, lovable, and soulfully created for everyone.

Vision

To become a trusted name in local fragrance — known for soulful blends, high-quality ingredients, and affordable luxury. We aim to build a community where every bottle tells a story, and where scent becomes a symbol of self-expression, confidence, and love.

🕯️ Amber — Warm, rich, sensual. Notes: Amber, labdanum, benzoin.

🌶️ Spicy — Exotic and bold. Notes: Cardamom, pepper, saffron.

🌸 Floral — Feminine and romantic. Notes: Rose, jasmine, peony.

🧼 Powdery — Soft and intimate. Notes: Iris, violet, white musk.

🍓 Fruity — Sweet and fresh. Notes: Strawberry, pear, mango.

🍭 Gourmand — Edible and comforting. Notes: Vanilla, caramel, coffee.

UNDERSTANDING PERFUME CONCENTRATIONS

Parfum / Extrait de Parfum — 20–30% oil, 8–12+ hours, deep and luxurious. Best for special occasions.

Eau de Parfum (EDP) — 15–20% oil, 6–8 hours, balanced and long-lasting. Great for daily wear.

Eau de Toilette (EDT) — 5–15% oil, 4–6 hours, fresh and light. Ideal for casual days.

Eau de Cologne (EDC) — 2–5% oil, 2–4 hours, very light. Good for layering or refreshing.

Body Mist / Splash — 1–3% oil, 1–2 hours, gentle scent. Best for post-shower or bedtime.

PERFUME STORAGE TIPS

✅ Store in a cool, dry place (away from sunlight and heat)

❌ Don’t leave perfumes in humid areas like bathrooms

✅ Keep the cap tightly closed to prevent evaporation

❄️ Optional: Refrigerate only if your room gets too warm — avoid temperature changes

HOW TO MAKE PERFUME LAST LONGER

On Skin: Apply on pulse points, moisturize first, don’t rub wrists, spray from 6–8 inches.

On Clothes: Spray on collars/sleeves, avoid silk, let air dry naturally.

BONUS TIPS

Spray on hairbrush, layer with lotions, carry travel atomizer.
